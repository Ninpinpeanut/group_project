What to do with a hedgehog?

Is it sick or injured?

Take the hedgehog to a vet immediately. Vets have a professional obligation to treat injured wildlife. If you are unable to take it yourself one of the following organisations may be able to collect it.

Roger's Wildlife Rescue based in Woodingdean. 01273 308268

East Sussex WRAS based in Whitesmith. 07815 078234

Is it something else?

The hedgehog must be helped if it is

  • Out in the daytime
  • Weighs less that 600g
  • Covered in ticks
If you find a hedgehog out during the winter, leave it alone unless it looks malnourished. If unsure, ring a rescue and ask for advice.
